We have compiled a list of the most frequently asked
questions from our clients before starting a project

1. Why do I need an architect?

An architect is a professional and expert in their field. Through their knowledge and experience, your architect can save you both time and money, and also give you peace of mind by taking the stress out of the whole building process. Your architect can also maximise your return on investment by adding value to your project.

2. What type of architecture do you specialise in?

Our primary expertise serves multi-residential, aged care, health, hospitality, and commercial sectors. However, we do not limit ourselves in the type of projects commissioned.

3. What type of clients do you currently work with?

We work with a diverse range of clients including but not limited to property developers, aged care facility managers, and commercial and hospitality professionals.

4. Who will work on my project?

A dedicated Project Leader will be assigned to manage your project from commencement through to completion. They will act as your representative on your behalf when dealing with third parties.

5. How long does the design process take?

As each individual project has its own requirements and parameters, the design process turnaround varies. We’d be happy to discuss your requirements with you to give you an estimated time frame.

6. What type of fees can I expect to pay?

We can tailor a solution to your budget and requirements. After meeting to discuss your initial design brief we will provide you with a written fee proposal. Depending on the services quoted for, fees are usually worked out on a percentage or lump sum basis or a mixture of both.

7. Can you design my new home build and/or extension?

We mainly serves the commercial sector. However, we have experience in designing luxury residential homes.

8. What type of clients do you currently work with?

We work with a diverse range of clients including but not limited to property developers, aged care facility managers, and commercial and hospitality professionals.

9. How long does the design process take?

As each individual project has its own requirements and parameters, the design process turnaround varies. We’d be happy to discuss your requirements with you to give you an estimated time frame.

10. Can you help me obtain a permit for my development?

We can arrange planning approval and building permits for your development.
